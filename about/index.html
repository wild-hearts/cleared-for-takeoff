About Cleared For Take-Off

Naomi Shiels · Founder

I spent years in commercial aviation, managing the systems and the people that get an aircraft safely off the ground. So when I started teaching people to use AI, the metaphor was already sitting there. You do not need to understand the engineering to be the captain. You need to know what the instruments are telling you, when to trust the crew, and that you are the one in command.

That is the whole idea behind Cleared For Take-Off. You are the Captain. The AI tools are your crew. They do the heavy lifting and teach you the controls, but they never fly the plane. You do.

Who I am

I am Naomi Shiels, a Canberra-based former linguist, lawyer and commercial aviation manager, and the founder of Wild Hearts Publishing. I have written 14 books and made 216 songs using AI, and I build with these tools every day.

Here is the part that matters most: I am not an engineer. I learned AI the same way you will, by showing up, trying things, failing at some of them, and keeping what worked. This course is what I kept. There is no secret technical background you are missing. There is just a tool that is genuinely new, and a calm way to learn it.

Why I made this

Everyone around you seems to be using AI, some brilliantly, most badly and not telling you. The people who are nervous about it are not slow. They are sensible, and they have been handed something new without a plain-English manual.

I got tired of watching capable adults feel left behind by a tool that, once you cut through the hype, is not that hard to use well. So I built the manual I wished existed: no jargon, no rush, no pretending to be someone you are not. Just a clear path from curious-and-cautious to a calm, confident weekly habit, on your own terms.

What I believe about AI

  • You stay in control. AI drafts, explains and organises. You decide. Every important call stays yours.
  • Shape with it, verify its facts. Use it freely to shape words, plans and ideas. Check anything it presents as a fact before you rely on it.
  • Honesty over hype. I will always tell you what AI does badly, not just what it does well. That is how you stay safe.
  • It is for everyone. Whatever your line of work, and whatever your age, this is learnable. You are on time, not late.

How to start

The first module of the course is completely free, with no payment details and no commitment. It is the calmest possible way to find out whether this is for you.
