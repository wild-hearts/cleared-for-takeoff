The flight plan in detail

What’s in each module

Click any module to see what’s inside.

1

Pre-Flight Checks Free

60–75 mins

“I don’t even know where to start, and everyone seems to already know.”We address this before a single lesson begins.

  • What AI actually is, and what it isn’t: three things AI does well, three it does badly
  • Choosing your first tool: ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini or Copilot, side by side
  • Signing up safely, with a privacy walkthrough (so it doesn’t train on you)
  • Your dashboard, your settings, your first hello

Cleared To TrySign up to one tool. Say hello to it. Ask it three things about itself.

2

Cleared For Taxi Free

75–90 mins

“I’m going to ask it the wrong thing and look stupid.”Spoiler: the AI doesn’t care, and neither do we.

  • Prompting in plain English: AI is not Google
  • The four-line prompt: context, task, format, tone
  • How to ask AI to ask YOU better questions
  • Specific vs vague, and when to start over
  • The week-one mistakes everyone makes
  • Saving and reusing the prompts that work

Cleared To TryPick one task you do every week. Get AI to help you with it. Compare the time spent.

3

Cleared For Takeoff

six everyday paths

“I’m worried I’ll become lazy, or stop thinking for myself.”AI does the typing. You still do the thinking.

  • Writing emails, letters and complaints
  • Summarising long documents
  • Planning anything
  • Research that doesn’t lie to you
  • Health information without panicking
  • Money and admin, including MyGov

Cleared To TryPick one task from any path. Do it with AI. Notice what changed.

4

In The Air

90–120 mins · one track

“Will I actually use this, or is it just a course I forget about?”This module is anchored in a real project.

  • Track A: Write something (a memoir, family history, eulogy, speech, blog, letter to the editor)
  • Track B: Start something (a small business idea, side project, Substack, community group)
  • Track C: Sort something (retirement, a renovation, a trip, an estate)
  • Track D: Learn something (a hobby, a language, a skill, AI as tutor)
  • Live cohort: a 30-minute coaching call to lock in your project

Cleared To TrySpend two hours on your track project this week.

5

Holding Pattern

75 mins

“I’ve heard horror stories. What if I get scammed or burned?”That fear is legitimate.

  • Hallucinations: confidently wrong vs quietly right
  • Scam awareness for Australians over 50: voice cloning, grandparent scams, romance scams, fake invoices, deepfakes
  • The Privacy Act in plain English
  • What never to put into AI
  • Trust, verify, walk away

Cleared To TryRun three “trust tests” on your tool. Catch it being wrong. Build the muscle.

6

Final Approach

60 mins

“Will I still be able to use this in six months when everything changes again?”Yes. The literacy travels.

  • Your weekly routine: one page, one habit, one tool
  • When NOT to use AI
  • Staying current without anxiety
  • Which paid tools are worth it, and which aren’t
  • Bringing someone along with you
  • A final reflection

Cleared To TryWrite your own one-page AI playbook for your life. Live cohort students share it in the community.
