**Document summary:** A comprehensive profile of Suno AI, the music generation tool, for Australian adults aged 50+. Suno lets anyone create original songs — complete with vocals, instrumentation, and lyrics — by typing a description. This profile covers what it is, pricing, use cases, limitations, and five music prompts tailored to this audience.

**Last updated:** May 2026

## What Is Suno AI and Who Makes It?

Suno is an AI music generation tool founded in 2022 by Mikey Shulman and a team of musicians and engineers in Cambridge, Massachusetts. The company's mission is to make music creation accessible to everyone — not just people who have spent years learning instruments or music theory.

Here is what Suno actually does: you type a description of a song, and within about 30 seconds it generates a complete, original piece of music with instruments, vocals, and professionally produced sound. You can specify the genre, the mood, the tempo, whether you want male or female vocals, whether there are lyrics, and what the lyrics should say.

The result is not a loop of pre-made sounds. Suno generates original music — melodies, harmonies, lyrics, and production — that does not exist anywhere else.

As of 2026, the quality of Suno's output is genuinely remarkable. At its best, Suno produces music that sounds like it was made by a real band in a real studio. At its average, it sounds like a competent indie artist. At its occasional worst, lyrics can be slightly awkward or vocals can mishandle certain consonants. But the average quality is well above what anyone expected AI to achieve in this medium.

## Free vs Paid Tiers (Pricing in AUD, May 2026)

*Note: Suno prices in USD. AUD figures are approximate based on May 2026 exchange rates.*

### Free Plan
- 50 credits per day
- 10 songs per day (each song generation costs 5 credits)
- Songs are publicly visible in Suno's explore gallery
- Non-commercial use only (cannot monetise the music)
- Credits reset daily; unused credits do not carry over

**Cost:** $0

### Pro Plan
- 2,500 credits per month (~500 songs)
- Commercial rights (you own the music and can sell it, use it in videos, monetise it on YouTube, etc.)
- Song Editor (edit generated lyrics and structure)
- Stem extraction (separate vocals from instruments)
- Priority generation (faster results)

**Cost:** USD $10/month = approximately AUD $16/month
or USD $8/month billed annually = approximately AUD $12.50/month

### Premier Plan
- 10,000 credits per month (~2,000 songs)
- Everything in Pro
- Suno Studio (AI-native music production environment)
- MIDI exports
- Multi-track editing
- Batch generation
- Priority support

**Cost:** USD $30/month = approximately AUD $47/month
or USD $24/month billed annually = approximately AUD $37.50/month

*Note: The free plan's 10 songs per day is sufficient for exploration and casual use. The Pro plan makes sense if you want commercial rights or regular production.*

## What It Is Genuinely Good At

**Complete songs from a text description.** The single most striking thing about Suno is that it generates complete, produced songs with words and music together. Not a backing track. Not a loop. An actual song with a beginning, verse, chorus, bridge, and ending.

**Genre versatility.** Suno handles an enormous range of genres: country, folk, blues, pop, rock, classical, jazz, bossa nova, gospel, Celtic, indie, electronic, hip-hop, metal, and many more. It handles Australian styles like bush ballads and folk rock. It handles era-specific sounds — a song that sounds like it was recorded in 1965, or one that sounds contemporary.

**Custom lyrics.** You can either let Suno generate lyrics from your description or write your own lyrics for it to set to music. This opens up extraordinary possibilities: setting your own poem to music, creating a song from a speech someone gave at a special occasion, or writing a personalised song as a gift.

**Birthday and celebration songs.** One of the most immediately practical use cases: type the name and a few details about someone's life and Suno generates a personalised birthday song in two minutes. There is nothing quite like giving someone a song written specifically about them.

**Instrumental music.** You can generate music without vocals — background music for a video, a peaceful piece for a relaxation recording, a theme tune for a community podcast.

**Processing and recreation of musical memories.** If you remember a song from your past — the vague feeling of it, the genre, the era — you can describe it and Suno can approximate something close. This is a remarkable creative experience.

## What It Struggles With

**Lyrics can occasionally be awkward.** At its best, Suno's AI-generated lyrics are surprisingly good. Occasionally, they stumble — a line that does not scan properly, a word that sounds odd when sung, a chorus that repeats too literally. If you care deeply about the words, write your own and feed them to Suno.

**Vocal consonants in fast passages.** Suno's vocals handle flowing, melodic passages beautifully. In rapid-fire sections with lots of consonants, the vocals can sound slightly blurred. Choose songs with measured tempos for the cleanest results.

**It cannot mimic specific real artists.** You cannot ask Suno to "make this sound like Paul Kelly" and get a reliable result. You can describe Paul Kelly's style ("Australian folk-rock, storytelling lyrics, acoustic guitar, spoken word sections, working-class characters") and get something in the same territory, but not a mimicry.

**Genre mixing can be inconsistent.** Asking for very unusual combinations ("a country song with electronic drums and a jazz trumpet") sometimes works and sometimes produces something confused. Keep genre descriptions coherent.

**No revision of existing songs in real time.** You cannot edit a generated song like you would edit text. You can re-generate or use the Song Editor (Pro feature) to adjust structure, but the process is different from text editing.

## Best Use Cases for Someone Aged 50+

1. **Personalised songs as gifts.** A 70th birthday song for a spouse or friend — listing specific memories, inside jokes, and life milestones — is genuinely one of the most meaningful and surprising gifts you can give. It takes about 10 minutes to create.

2. **Memorial and tribute music.** Create an original piece for a funeral service, a wake, or a memorial — something that captures the person's character or their favourite musical style. Far more personal than selecting a generic song from Spotify.

3. **Family gathering entertainment.** Prepare a series of songs for a family reunion, a anniversary party, or a retirement celebration — personalised music featuring family members' names, shared memories, and inside references.

4. **Background music for slideshows and videos.** Creating a video slideshow of holiday photos, family memories, or a tribute requires music that fits the mood. Suno generates the exact length and tone you need, with no copyright complications.

5. **Creative and personal expression.** If you have always wanted to write and record a song but never had the musical skills — here is your chance. Your ideas, your words, your vision — Suno provides the music.

6. **Reconnecting with music you love.** If you love a particular genre — bush ballads, 1960s folk, Delta blues, Irish traditional — Suno lets you generate as much new music in that style as you want, exploring a musical world you love.

7. **Writing and setting poems to music.** If you write poetry, Suno can set it to music. Paste your poem, specify the musical style, and hear it transformed.

## How to Get Started: Step by Step

1. Go to **https://www.suno.com** and click "Sign up"
2. Create an account with your email, or sign in with Google
3. You will arrive at the Suno interface. Click "Create" in the left sidebar.
4. You will see two options:
   - **Song Description** (Suno writes everything): Type a description of what you want
   - **Custom** (you write the lyrics): Write your own lyrics and describe the style separately

**For your first song (Song Description mode):**
1. In the Song Description box, type something specific. Not just "a happy song" — give it detail.
2. Example: "An upbeat folk song about gardening in the Australian summer, acoustic guitar and harmonica, female vocals, hopeful and joyful, with a chorus about finding peace in simple things"
3. Click "Create" — Suno generates two versions
4. Press play on each and listen
5. Click the three dots next to a song to download it, share it, or open it in the editor

**For a personalised birthday song:**
1. Choose "Custom" mode
2. In the Lyrics box, write something like:
   ```
   [Verse 1]
   Here's to Margaret, seventy today
   Started in Ballarat, found her own way
   Three kids and a garden and a lifetime of love
   Seventy years is never enough

   [Chorus]
   Happy birthday Margaret, here's your song
   All of us love you, been that way all along
   ```
3. In the Style box, type: "Warm folk-pop, acoustic guitar, female vocals, celebratory"
4. Click Create

## Privacy and Data Considerations (Australian Law)

**Who owns the music?** This is important. On the free plan, music is non-commercial and Suno's terms grant you limited personal rights only. On the Pro and Premier plans, you own the generated music and have full commercial rights to use, sell, and monetise it. Read the current terms of service at suno.com before using generated music commercially.

**Public gallery.** Free plan songs appear in Suno's public Explore gallery by default. Pro and Premier users can generate private songs.

**Data collection.** Suno collects your account information and usage data. Your prompts and the generated music may be used to improve Suno's models.

**US company.** Suno is a US company; Australian individuals are governed by Suno's terms of service. The Australian Privacy Act applies to Australian organisations using Suno commercially.

**Copyright considerations.** Music created by Suno is generated by AI and does not infringe existing copyrights (it does not sample or copy existing recordings). However, AI-generated music's copyright status is still being clarified in various jurisdictions, including Australia. As of 2026, the position in Australia is that AI-generated work without sufficient human authorship may not attract copyright protection. If you want to be clear about ownership for commercial purposes, use the Pro plan and document your creative involvement in the lyrics and direction.

## Honest Assessment: Is It Worth It?

The free plan (10 songs per day) is more than enough to discover whether Suno is for you. Spend an afternoon generating songs in your favourite genres and experimenting with personalised lyrics. If it brings you joy, subscribe to Pro.

The Pro plan at approximately AUD $16/month is excellent value for anyone who wants to:
- Give personalised music as gifts
- Create music for videos or slideshows
- Explore musical creativity as a hobby
- Produce original music for personal or commercial use

If you have any love of music at all — listening to it, having it in your life, feeling moved by it — Suno is worth exploring. The ability to generate a song that perfectly captures a feeling or honours a specific person is one of the most emotionally resonant uses of AI available.

**Verdict:** One of the most joyful and genuinely surprising tools in this series. The creative possibilities for personal expression and gifting are significant. Free plan is generous. Recommended for anyone who loves music and wants to create rather than just listen.
