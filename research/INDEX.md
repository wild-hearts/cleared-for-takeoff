## Section 2: Tool Profiles

Each profile is 1,500-2,000 words covering: what it is and who makes it, free vs paid pricing in AUD (May 2026), genuine strengths, genuine weaknesses, best use cases for adults 50+, step-by-step setup, five tailored prompts, privacy and Australian law considerations, and an honest verdict.

### `/research/tool-profiles/tool-profile-chatgpt.md`
ChatGPT by OpenAI. The world's most used AI assistant. Free tier and Plus (AUD ~$35/month). Best for writing assistance, health research preparation, travel planning, and voice conversations. Strong starter tool for beginners.

### `/research/tool-profiles/tool-profile-claude.md`
Claude by Anthropic. Distinguished by high-quality writing, long document analysis, and honest uncertainty. Free tier and Pro (AUD ~$31/month). Best for serious writing, reading complex documents, and nuanced conversation.

### `/research/tool-profiles/tool-profile-gemini.md`
Google Gemini. Stands out for deep Google ecosystem integration: summarises Gmail, searches Google Photos, works with Google Lens camera feature. Free tier and AI Pro (AUD $29.99/month). Best for heavy Google users.

### `/research/tool-profiles/tool-profile-copilot.md`
Microsoft Copilot. Built into Windows 11, Microsoft Edge, Word, and Outlook. Free and Pro (~AUD $30-36/month). Best for Windows/Microsoft Office users who want AI without a new app. Includes warning about the Recall feature.

### `/research/tool-profiles/tool-profile-perplexity.md`
Perplexity AI. AI-powered search engine that shows sources for every claim. Free (5 Pro Searches/day) and Pro (~AUD $31/month billed in USD). The most trustworthy tool for health research, government entitlements, and fact-checking.

### `/research/tool-profiles/tool-profile-meta-ai.md`
Meta AI in WhatsApp and Facebook. Already on the phone of every WhatsApp user — no setup required. Free (Meta One Plus/Premium in testing). Best as a zero-barrier first AI experience. Significant privacy considerations covered honestly.

### `/research/tool-profiles/tool-profile-canva-ai.md`
Canva Magic Studio. Australian-built design tool with 25+ AI features. Free (50 AI credits/month) and Pro (AUD $169.99/year). Best for cards, family history books, community publications, and creative projects. Background Remover, Dream Lab, Magic Write covered in detail.

### `/research/tool-profiles/tool-profile-grammarly.md`
Grammarly AI. Writing assistant that works inside Gmail, Facebook, Word, and any browser text box. Free (100 AI prompts/month) and Pro (~AUD $18/month annually). Best for confident correspondence, catching errors, and tone adjustment.

### `/research/tool-profiles/tool-profile-midjourney.md`
Midjourney. Premium AI image generation. Basic plan ~AUD $16/month. Produces the most artistically impressive AI images available. Best for people with visual creative interests: family history illustration, original artwork, cards, and prints.

### `/research/tool-profiles/tool-profile-suno.md`
Suno AI. AI music generation. Free (10 songs/day) and Pro (~AUD $16/month). Generates complete songs — vocals, instruments, lyrics — from a text description. Best for personalised birthday songs, memorial music, creative expression.

## Section 3: Mini-Courses

Each mini-course is a complete five-lesson script, approximately 3,500-4,500 words, structured for:
- NotebookLM import (clear headings, summaries, well-organised)
- Video recording (conversational tone, clear structure)
- Audio/podcast production (or NotebookLM Audio Overview generation)

Structure of each course:
- Title, summary, and audience statement
- Learning outcomes (3-5 bullet points)
- Lesson 1: What it is and why it matters (~600 words)
- Lesson 2: Setup step by step (~500 words)
- Lesson 3: Five practical sessions/creations (~700 words)
- Lesson 4: What nobody tells you — limitations and gotchas (~500 words)
- Lesson 5: Building it into daily life (~400 words)
- Quick reference card with prompt formulas and cheat sheet

**Claude: The AI That Reads Carefully and Writes Well.** Emphasises document analysis, quality writing, and honest research. Includes a ChatGPT vs Claude comparison table. Best for learners with significant correspondence or family history projects.

### `/research/mini-courses/mini-course-gemini.md`
**Google Gemini: Your AI That Lives Inside Google.** Focuses specifically on Gmail integration, Google Photos search, and Google Lens camera use. Privacy section addresses the Google data tradeoff directly.

**Perplexity AI: The Research Tool That Shows Its Sources.** Emphasises the importance of citation and verification. Structured around health research, government entitlements, and fact-checking. Includes a guide to reading and evaluating Perplexity results.

### `/research/mini-courses/mini-course-meta-ai.md`

## Using This Package

### For Course Development

The tool profiles provide the research foundation; the mini-courses provide the scripted content. Each mini-course is structured to be:
- Recorded as a video lesson
- Delivered as a live presentation
- Converted to audio via NotebookLM
- Adapted as a written blog post series

### For NotebookLM

Upload any mini-course file as a source in NotebookLM. Generate an Audio Overview. Use the 10 example questions in the NotebookLM guide as your starting queries.

### For Updating

All pricing information is noted as May 2026. AI tools change rapidly. At minimum, pricing should be reviewed every 6 months. Tool features and capabilities should be reviewed annually.
