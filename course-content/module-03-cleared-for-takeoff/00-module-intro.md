# Module 3, Intro: Cleared For Takeoff

**Spoken duration:** ~3 minutes  
**Word count (spoken):** ~420 words  
**Format:** Direct to camera  
**On-screen text:** Module title; path menu display  

---

[LOOK TO CAMERA]

There is a concern I hear a lot from people at this stage of the course.

[PAUSE]

"What if I start using AI for everything, and my brain goes soft? What if I forget how to write, or plan, or think through problems myself?"

[PAUSE]

It is a fair question. And I want to address it directly before we go any further.

[PAUSE]

AI does not think for you. It responds to you. The thinking — what you need, what matters to you, what your actual situation is, what good looks like — all of that still comes from you. The AI has no idea. It can only work with what you give it.

[PAUSE]

The people I've seen use AI well are not less capable than they were before. They are more capable. They spend less time on the parts of a task they found tedious, and more time on the parts that actually require them — the judgment, the relationships, the decisions that are genuinely theirs to make.

[PAUSE]

Using a calculator does not make you worse at maths. It makes you faster at the part that is purely arithmetic, so you can spend more energy on the part that requires actual thought.

[PAUSE]

AI is a tool. It is your tool. You decide what to point it at.

[PAUSE]

[ON SCREEN: Module 3 path menu]

This module is different from the first two. Instead of working through lessons together, you are going to choose your paths.

There are six paths in this module. Each one covers a specific type of task. You are going to pick three of them — the three that are most relevant to your actual life right now.

The paths are:

Path A: Writing emails and letters
Path B: Summarising documents
Path C: Planning — trips, events, projects
Path D: Research and fact-finding
Path E: Health information
Path F: Money and admin

[BACK TO CAMERA]

[PAUSE]

You do not need to do all six. Pick the three that feel most immediately useful, or the three that represent tasks you currently find difficult or time-consuming.

Each path is a standalone session. You can come back to the others later.

[PAUSE]

Let's go.
